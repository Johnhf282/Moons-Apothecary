Meet Cristina

Dedication to Natural Healing

Cristina is dedicated to bringing you the finest in organic and natural remedies. With a deep-rooted commitment to wellness and holistic health, Cristina’s journey began with a profound belief in the healing power of nature.

Her expertise spans a range of natural therapies, from herbal medicine to essential oils, all curated with care and precision. Cristina’s approach combines traditional wisdom with modern practices, ensuring each product supports both physical and emotional well-being.

Our Philosophy

Cristina’s passion for natural healing is evident in every aspect of Moons Apothecary. Her philosophy is grounded in the belief that nature provides the most effective solutions for health and wellness. She is dedicated to sourcing high-quality, organic ingredients that are ethically harvested and sustainably produced.

Every product is crafted with care, reflecting Cristina’s commitment to purity, effectiveness, and sustainability. Her hands-on approach ensures that each remedy meets the highest standards, making natural wellness accessible to everyone.
